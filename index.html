Bedlam

Bedlam is a sound and a noise synthesizer set for Android tablets.

The app contains three different synthesizers:

- A drum synth

- A sensor synth

- A Slider synth

Bedlam at Google Play.

GigSearch

Search gigs by a city or an artist from the Last.fm API. The user can view the artist information (through the city search) and also the event information (through the artist search).

GigSearch at Windows Store.

Tampere Bus Stalker

Tampere Bus Stalker is a bus position and timetable viewer app.

Follow the busses of Tampere on a map and view detailed information about them. The user also gets information about the surroundings (the city).

Tampere Bus Stalker at Google Play.
